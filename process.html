The Science Behind Your Transformation

ResetRx focuses on three clinically validated biomarkers that are strong predictors of all-cause mortality - one of the most comprehensive indicators of long-term health and longevity.

C-Reactive Protein

Inflammation Marker

Measures systemic inflammation levels in your body. High CRP levels are linked to increased risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic conditions.

HbA1c

Blood Sugar Control

Indicates your average blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months. Critical for preventing diabetes and metabolic dysfunction.

Total Cholesterol

Cardiovascular Health

Essential for assessing cardiovascular risk and overall heart health. Proper cholesterol levels support longevity and vitality.
