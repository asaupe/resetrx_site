Your Body's Report Card

Why do some 60-year-olds look and feel 40 while others seem decades older?

The Science:

Your biomarkers tell the real story of your health—they're like a dashboard showing how well your body is functioning at the cellular level. Five key markers reveal your healthspan trajectory: Hs-CRP measures inflammation, HbA1c shows your 3-month glucose control, Cholesterol levels indicate cardiovascular health, triglycerides reflect metabolic efficiency and cortisol reveals stress response.

These aren't just markers with numbers—they're predictors of how you'll age. Research shows people with optimal biomarker profiles live 10+ years longer with better quality of life.
